Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering and Delivery

How do I place an order?
To place an order, simply browse our menu and add items to your cart. When you're ready to check out, follow the prompts to enter your payment and delivery information.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the time of day. Our estimated delivery times are displayed during checkout, and you will receive updates on the status of your order via email or text message.

Payment and Billing

What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, as well as PayPal and Apple Pay.
How will I be charged for my order?
Your payment will be processed at the time of checkout, and you will receive a receipt via email.

Returns and Refunds

What is your return policy?
We do not accept returns on food items, but if there is a problem with your order, please contact us and we will do our best to resolve the issue.
What happens if my order is incorrect or damaged?
If there is a problem with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery and we will work with you to make it right.

Need more help? Contact our customer support team at support@myfooddeliverywebsite.com.
